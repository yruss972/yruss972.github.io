Here is a fairly short regular expression you can use to match valid IPV4 IP addresses:  
/^(((25\[0-4])|(2[0-4\]\[0-9\])|(1?\[0-9]?[0-9]))\.){3}((25[0-4])|(2[0-4\]\[0-9\])|(1?[0-9]?[0-9]))$/

Good luck!
